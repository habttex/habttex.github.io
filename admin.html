About Me

The Person Behind the Data

My name is Habte. I specialize in collecting and verifying Ethiopian business data, especially in the coffee export sector — one of Africa's most valuable commodity markets.

I help companies save time by providing clean, structured, and ready-to-use supplier lists. No more chasing dead ends or unverified contacts.
